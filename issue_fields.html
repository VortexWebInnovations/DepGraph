Issue fields in Dependency Graph for Jira Cloud

The Jira Dependency Graph Addon now offers a game-changing opportunity – the ability to select issue fields that matter most to your project's dependency management. This empowers you with unprecedented control over how you visualize and track dependencies within your Jira projects.

By selecting these issue fields, you can customize your dependency graph to provide invaluable insights into your project's status, timeline, and team dynamics. Visualize dependencies alongside progress updates, monitor time allocation, and stay on top of critical due dates. Assignees and teams are easily identifiable, streamlining collaboration and accountability.

This opportunity to choose issue fields transforms your dependency graph into a versatile and powerful tool, making your Jira project management more efficient and insightful than ever. Embrace this chance to customize and optimize your project management strategies and elevate your Jira experience to new heights.
