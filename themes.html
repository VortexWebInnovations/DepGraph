Themes of Dependency Graph for Jira Cloud

The Jira Dependency Graph Addon now offers users the exciting opportunity to personalize their project management experience with a selection of captivating themes. With themes like Jira, Dark, Forest, Violet, and Neutral, you can infuse your dependency graph with a visual style that resonates most with your project's character.

These diverse themes not only enhance the visual appeal of your dependency graph but also reflect your personal or project-specific preferences. By choosing a theme that resonates with you, you can create a more engaging and user-friendly environment, making project management in Jira an even more enjoyable and efficient experience.

By default Dependency Graph installs "Jira" theme

The Jira theme embodies the classic Jira aesthetic, seamlessly integrating the dependency graph with your existing Jira environment.

"Dark" theme for Dependency graph

The Dark theme lends a sleek and modern appeal, perfect for those who prefer a more subdued visual experience.

"Forest" theme for Dependency graph

For nature enthusiasts, the Forest theme brings the outdoors to your project management, creating a refreshing and calming atmosphere.

"Violet" theme for Dependency graph

The Violet theme adds a touch of elegance and vibrancy, ideal for those who appreciate a pop of color in their workspace.

"Neutral" theme for Dependency graph

And if you favor a minimalist approach, the Neutral theme offers a clean and uncluttered look, allowing you to focus on the essentials.
